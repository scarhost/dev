(VALUES)

  1. 01 SENSIBILITY

    It is important to refine our senses by maintaining a strong curiosity, pursuing new life experiences, and dedicating ourselves to constant self-improvement. This develops a core set of beliefs, and when we live, in word and deed, aligned with our own principles, we can then begin to discern the true essence of the world.

  2. 02 HUMANITY

    Our love goes out to people who treat others with gentleness, respect, and humor. And only in demonstrating compassion to those with different perspectives and insights can we achieve the mutual understanding that builds strong and resilient teams.

  3. 03 TENACITY

    Exceeding others’ expectations requires pushing yourself to never be complacent in the middle of the journey, and never, ever giving up, no matter how difficult the process. Extra effort is necessary for the skillful execution of creative ideas, and this requires a mindset that relishes the entire journey and aims to follow through to the very end.
